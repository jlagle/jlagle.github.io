Outline for Client Project

Project Overview:

Description and Purpose: This application will be based around a UNCC dance club called Move. Its purpose will be to advertise and promote the club as well as be updated with events, new information, and other potential media. This can also serve as a way to get in conteact with the Move mebers and even go as far as serving as a potential sign up website.

Users: The intended users of this website are students who want to get involved with Move, clients seeking Move members to potentially perform in their own companies, or anyone seeking inspiration or an outlet for dancing.

Content: This website will feature images, videos, and other media to represent the club and its past showcases. There will also be information and bios about the Move board members and its staff advisor. There will be contact, showcase, and audition imformation as well as upcoming dates.
