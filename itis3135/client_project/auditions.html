Auditions

A FAQ page and general information on how we hold auditions.

FAQ

  • When are auditions hosted? We host auditions in the first few weeks of every semester.
  • Are you open to all styles of dance? Yes! We love to have a diverse set of dances in our showcase.
  • How do I get an audition? You sign up through our Google Form link or in the Sign-up Page
  • Do I need to bring anything? Nope! Just bring your energy and excitement!
