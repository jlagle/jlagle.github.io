Across these pages you can find collections of my favorite shots of various flowers, plants, animals, and insects. I've always loved the beauty of nature (specifically flowers) and want to try to capture it and share it with others. I hope you enjoy!

About

I have been passionate about nature photography for several years. Capturing close-ups, wildlife, and the subtle details of the outdoors has become my creative outlet.

I use photography as an outlet to express myself and show people what I also enjoy. It is also a stress reliever and a fun activity I love to do in my free time.

Best Times

When starting I had to be patient and experiment with different times of day to find out how to take pictures I like.

Best Locations

My favorite and most reliable spot I have found are the botanical gardens at UNC Charlotte. The gardens are easily accessible, close to me, and always have amazing flowers and wildlife.

I hope in the future I get more time to explore more areas and find cooler places to take pictures at.

How

I unfortunately can not afford my own camera, but instead I rent a camera from the UNCC library. I use the Canon EOS Rebel T7i 2 Lens Kit with lenses of 18-55mm and 55-250mm.

It takes experimenting and a lot of patience to figure out how and when to use certain lenses and how to get the correct depth, focus, and zoom.

Why

Nature photography allows me to capture and visualize the world around me. It helps me take snapshots in time and share them with others.

Sharing these photos lets others see the beauty I experience.
